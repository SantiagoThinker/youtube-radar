> - _"AI is changing everything" — generic framings_
> - _Basic RAG / fine-tuning / prompt engineering explanations_
> - _"Distribution beats technology" — startup truisms_
> - _Generic "how to be a great PM" career advice_
> - _AGI timeline predictions_

What **does** count as a valuable insight:
- Concrete numbers (revenue, conversion, latency, cost) from practitioners
- Names of companies / people to subscribe to or contact, with a reason why
- Counter-intuitive observations backed by specific evidence
- Tactical playbooks ("how exactly to do X")
- Early-market signals — what emerged in the last 3 months

When in doubt — skip the bullet, don't stretch.
